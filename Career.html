Career in the US

Luka flew to America for the 2018/19 season and was selected as the third choice at the draft by the Atlanta Hawks, who than traded him with Trae Young to the Dallas Mavericks.

He still plays there and is their best player. He is so strong because of his unnatural talent and because of his incredible basketball intelligence. Above that his offensive skills are really impressive as he combines physical strength and outstanding shooting accuracy. His capabilities of guiding a team is the main reason he is considered one of the best players around wich to build a team.
