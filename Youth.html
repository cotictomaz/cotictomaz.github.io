Luka's youth

Dončic was born in Ljubljana, the capital city of Slovenia. A really small country with a little more than two million habitants. His father was a former basketball player and coach, so Dončic's first contact with a basketball ball was pretty early. He first started playing at age seven and fastly proved that he was a prodigee in the making. The young ascending star made himself known in the basketball world and caught the attention of one of the best clubs in Europe: Real Madrid. In the spanish capital his rise and his professional career began.
